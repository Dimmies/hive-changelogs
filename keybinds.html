Commands

Command Example Action
/cash /cash Check your cash amount
/givecash [id] [amount] /givecash 1 1000 Give another player money
/job /job Check what Job/Rank you have
/id /id Check you current Server ID
/chips /chips Check how many Casino Chips you have
/e [emote] /e dance Plays an emote
/emotebind [key] [emote] /emotebind num4 dance Binds an emote to a key (num4, num5, num6, num7. num8, num9)
/emotebinds /emotebinds Check your current bound emotes
/emotemenu /emotemenu Open the emote menu
/unbindemote [key] /unbindemote num4 Unbinds and emote from a key (num4, num5, num6, num7. num8, num9)
/binds /binds Open command binding menu
/bug /bug Opens the Bug Report UI
/me [msg] /me Reaches in Glovebox Shows a 3D me message
/givekeys [id] /givekeys 1 Gives the keys to the current vehicle to the specified player
/engine /engine Toggles the engine of the current vehicle
/seat /seat Moves you to the specified seat of a vehicle
/clear /clear Clears the chat (for yourself)
/ooc [msg] /ooc I'm cuffed in the prison! Sends a global Out of Character message in chat (Use this sparingly!)
/h or /hat /h or /hat Toggle Helmet/Hat
/b or /glasses /b or /glasses Toggle Glasses
/m or /mask /m or /mask Toggle Mask
/createrace [amount] /createrace 150 Start a street race with the specified buy-in
/stoprace /stoprace Stops the race you created
/quitrace /quitrace Leaves the current street race (you will not be refunded)
/startrace /startrace Starts the current street race
/givehousekey /givehousekey Gives a house key of the nearest owned house to the nearest player
/housekeys /housekeys View who owns keys to your houses
/decorate /decorate Decorate your home
/createhouse [price] [tier] /createhouse 35000 1 Creates a house at the current location (Real Estate Only)
/addgarage /addgarage Adds a garage to the current house (Real Estate Only)
/extras /extras Opens the extras/livery menu (Police/EMS/Mechanics Only)
/livery [id] /livery 2 Sets the livery of your vehicle (Police/EMS/Mechanics Only)
/jail /jail Opens the Jail Input Menu (Police Only)
/unjail [id] /unjail 1 Unjails the specified player (Police Only)
/setjail [id] [time] /setjail 1 15 Sets the jail time of the specified player (Police Only)
/fine /fine Opens the Fine Input Menu (Police Only)
/evidence [casid] /evidence 69 Opens the evidence stash of the specified Case ID (Police Only)
/clearblood /clearblood Clears the area of blood evidence (Police Only)
/takedna /takedna Take a DNA sample of the specified player (Police Only)
/uncuff [id] /uncuff 1 Uncuff the specified player (Police Only)
